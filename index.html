Welcome to Open APEX

Open APEX is an open-source initiative dedicated to building a low-cost, 3D-printed 6-axis robotic arm for education and research. We focus on modular design, IoT-enabled extensions, and accessible robotics solutions for students, educators, and developers worldwide.

Our Mission

We believe in democratizing robotics by providing affordable, open, and customizable solutions. By combining mechanical innovation with AI and IoT, Open APEX empowers users to explore advanced robotics without the barrier of high cost.

Get Involved

Want to contribute? Check out our GitHub repository, share your designs, or join the community to collaborate on exciting robotics projects. Together, we can shape the future of open robotics.
